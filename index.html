EvilESP_Flasher

EVILESP_Flasher is a browser-based firmware deployment interface for ESP-class devices.

This tool allows you to flash custom binaries directly from your browser using WebSerial technology — no drivers, no external flashing tools.

Select your firmware, connect your device via USB, and initiate flashing with a single click.

Designed for research, development, and educational purposes. Use only on hardware you own or are authorized to test.
